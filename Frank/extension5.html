Homestay

For all the animal lovers out there visiting God’s own country; here is a good news for you!!!All those who have a heart for animals, have innumerous opportunities in FRANK to pass some time with our lovable animals. FRANK arranges a homestay for all the tourists who wish to spend some quality time with the rescued animals.

This place is very eco-friendly and clean. It is surrounded by river on 3 sides. This charming, waterfront property offers an accommodation to suit an array of travel preferences. An air-conditioned room is provided. In addition to this, they are free to volunteer us in our rescue operations. They will be greeted by a bunch of dogs, cats, goats, rabbits, guinea pigs, hens and ducks. This place is truly a heaven for animal lovers and it’s going to be one of the most memorable holiday experiences…
